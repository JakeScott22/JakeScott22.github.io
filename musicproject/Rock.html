Inventors of Rock

Unlike rap, Rock and Roll had many creators, this includes people like Little Richard, Elvis Presley, Buddy Holly, Jerry Lee Lewis, Bo Diddly and more! Rock is a popular genre of music that originated in the 1940's and early 1950's. It was inspired by African American music styles such as gospel and jazz. In the earliest rock and roll styles, either the piano or saxaphone were the leading instrument. Classic rock and roll is usually played with one or two electric guitars a double bass or string bass, and a drum kit.

Rock and Roll Musicians of Today

Since the 1950's rock has evolved multiple times. New beats have been made, new instruments are being utilized in songs, and many other rock and rollers are now in the spotlight. A few bands that deserve praise are Linkin Park, Avenged Sevenfold, Thirty Second to Mars, and Three Days Grace.
