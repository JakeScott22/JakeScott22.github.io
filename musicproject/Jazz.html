Heart of Jazz

Jazz was created in the 1900s in New Orleans: The Melting Pot of Sound.It was created when their was a blend of music such as church music, ragtime, echoes of traditional African dumming, and more were all played. The man called The Jazz Original was the first big time Jazz musician. improvisation is the biggest defining feature of Jazz , this is to make up or create music as you go along. Jazz musicians play from printed music and they improvise solos.

Sme popular types of Jazz include Acid Jazz, Big Band, Blue Note, Cool, Crossover Jazz, Dixieland, and so much more. Some popular muscisians include Miles Davis, John Coltrane, Charlie Parker, Duke Ellington, Buddy Rich, CLifford Brown and so much more. Jazz music is highly popular in New Orleans, Chicago, and New York.
